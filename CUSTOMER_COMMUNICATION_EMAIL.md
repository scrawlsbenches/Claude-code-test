## FAQ Responses (For Follow-up Questions)

### Q: "Why didn't you catch these issues earlier?"

**A**: Our testing DID catch them—that's the purpose of our final pre-launch code review. This is our quality process working as designed. We've learned we should run this comprehensive review earlier in the dev cycle (after Sprint 3, not just before launch), and we're implementing that change for future releases.

---

### Q: "How confident are you in the February 6 date?"

**A**: 85% confident. We have 15% buffer time built into Phases 3-4. We will NOT launch until all quality gates pass, even if it means delaying to February 20. We'd rather under-promise and over-deliver than make another date commitment we can't keep.

---

### Q: "What happens if we can't wait until February?"

**A**: I understand timeline pressures. Let's discuss your specific needs:

- If you need deployment automation by year-end, we can recommend alternative solutions for the interim
- If February works but you need budget approval now, we can provide a Letter of Intent with the February launch date
- If you're evaluating multiple vendors, we're happy to remain in consideration for your Q1 2026 decision

What's driving your timeline requirement?

---

### Q: "Are you offering any compensation for the delay?"

**A**: For pilot customers, we're offering extended trial credits and early access to the February launch. For new customers, we're committed to delivering a significantly higher quality product—that's the compensation. Would extended trial terms or a discounted first-year rate help with your decision process?

---

### Q: "What if your competitors launch first?"

**A**: Our research shows no credible competitors launching before Q2 2026. Vendor A launched in Q2 2025 but uses legacy architecture. Vendor B is in alpha with a Q3 2026 target. The market is undersupplied—quality differentiation matters more than being first.

---

### Q: "Can we get access before February to test in our environment?"

**A**: Yes! We're hosting a beta preview event on January 20, 2026 for select customers. You'd get hands-on access to validate the security fixes and test in a staging environment. Interested? I'll send a calendar invite.

---

### Q: "How do we know you won't delay again?"

**A**: Fair question. Here's our accountability plan:

1. **Weekly progress updates** (every Friday) showing issues resolved vs. planned
2. **Phase gate reviews** (4 total) with Go/No-Go criteria
3. **External security audit** validation by independent third party
4. **Beta preview** on January 20 for you to validate readiness

If we're not ready by February 6, we'll communicate that by January 23 (2 weeks notice).
